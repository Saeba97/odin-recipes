the usual

this is the usual, which is a meal I make almost every day.

it consists of a ground meat or meat substitute, frozen vegetable medley, frozen peppers, and some type of seasoning.

ingredients

  • ground meat or substitute
  • frozen vegetable medley (peas, corn, grean beans)
  • frozen peppers
  • frozen cauliflower rice
  • seasoning

directions

  1. cook the meat base until almost fully cooked
  2. add in seasoning
  3. add in the vegetables
  4. when vegetables are cooked enough turn off heat
  5. eat
